FAQ

We're here to answer your questions about our courier services. Browse our FAQ section to learn more about our services, policies, and procedures, and contact us if you have any additional questions.

Pretoria CBD, Hatfield, Midrand, Randburg, Johannesburg CBD

We can deliver any document of your choice, provided we are given clear and descriptive communication on how to get the delivery done.

We exclusively provide same-day delivery services.

Our operational hours are limited to working hours, and we are unable to provide courier services outside of these hours. After-hours requests may be submitted until 5pm on Monday through Saturday. On Sundays and public holidays, requests may be submitted until 12pm.

Once your trip has been logged, you will receive a link to track your parcel in real-time. This ensures that you are always up-to-date on the status of your delivery and can plan accordingly..

We provide a tamper sticker upon document collection, and a second sticker for return trips to ensure document integrity.

If a filing request is received prior to 10am, the corresponding document will be delivered to the designated location no later than 1pm, provided that said location is within reasonable proximity. However, if the distance is significant, the document will be delivered to the preferred return drop-off location by the end of the day. Filing requests received between 10am and 2pm will also be delivered to the preferred return drop-off location by the end of the day. It is important to note that the filing request deadline is at 2pm, unless the location is within a 20km radius.

In the event that we are unable to file your documents, we consider it an unsuccessful filing. If this occurs, we will return the original document to you, along with a full report outlining the issue so that you can take appropriate action to rectify it.

We offer discounts to our regular customers who complete a certain number of courier trips over multiple weeks. Additionally, for high-volume orders of documents, we provide discounted rates to help you save on your courier expenses.
